About the RiskIQ Model
Technical documentation for the underlying AI architecture
🤖 Model Architecture: LightGBM
Gradient Boosting Decision Tree (GBDT) · Microsoft Research
🤖 LightGBM · GBDT

The RiskIQ Dashboard is powered by LightGBM, a high-performance, distributed gradient boosting framework developed by Microsoft. Specifically designed for speed and efficiency on large-scale tabular data.

TECHNIQUE 01
Leaf-wise Tree Growth
Chooses the leaf with max delta loss — lower loss and higher accuracy than level-wise methods.
TECHNIQUE 02
GOSS Sampling
Focuses on high-gradient instances, cutting computation time without sacrificing precision.
TECHNIQUE 03
Exclusive Feature Bundling
Bundles sparse, mutually exclusive features to reduce dimensionality and speed up training.

🚀 Growth Strategy
Leaf-wise vs Level-wise

Standard models grow trees row by row. RiskIQ's LightGBM grows by identifying paths that reduce error most aggressively (Leaf-wise). Requires careful max_depth tuning, but captures far more complex, non-linear financial relationships.

⚖️ Ethical AI Pipeline
Fairness by design

Protected classes (gender, age in sensitive contexts, ethnicity) are strictly excluded during feature engineering. We use SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) during validation to ensure decisions rely solely on legitimate financial factors.
